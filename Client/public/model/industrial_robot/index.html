In Animation Mode

Click any of the left buttons with number to run different stages of industrial robot animation.
Click on the button with cycled arrow to stop all animations and reset robot position.
Click on any color circle in the top left corner of the screen to change the color of the interface.
"Switch to" button switches between Free and Animated mode of the application.

In Free Mode

Click any part of the robot to enable editing menu and show the moving/rotation triggers.
Click on the colored buttons in the right bottom corner to change color of the selected part.
Click on any type of the nozzles in the left bottom corner of the screen to connect it to the robot.
This button switches between individual part or the whole robot coloring mode.
